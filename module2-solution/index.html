Guitar

The guitar is a fretted musical instrument that usually has six strings. It is typically played with both hands by plucking the strings with either a guitar pick or fingers of one hand .

Piano

The piano is an acoustic, stringed musical instrument invented in Italy by Bartolomeo Cristofori around the year 1700. It is played using keyboard, which is a row of keys that the performer presses down with the fingers.

Drum

The drum is a member of the percussion group of musical instruments . Drums consis of atleast one membrane , called drumhead that is stretched over a shell and struck to produce sound .
